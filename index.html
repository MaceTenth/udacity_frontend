What is Bladesmithing?

Bladesmithing is the art of making knives, swords, and blades using a forge, hammer, anvil, and smithing tools. The art of bladesmithing is thousands of years old and found in cultures all over the world. Bladesmiths use various metalworking techniques to forge blades, woodworking skills for knife and sword handles, and leatherworking knowledge to make sheaths.
